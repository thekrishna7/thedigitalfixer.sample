Our Humble Journey

Founded with a simple vision to bridge the educational gap in smaller towns, The Digital Fixer started by installing interactive panels in coaching classes in Morena. Recognizing that schools needed robust databases alongside classroom hardware, we expanded to offer custom School ERP portals and Student mobile applications.

Today, we have grown into a multi-city agency with offices in Ambah, Morena, and Gurugram, servicing clients in Bhopal, Indore, Delhi NCR, Jaipur, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and worldwide.
